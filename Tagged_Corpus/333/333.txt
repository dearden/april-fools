We've been dreaming about the return of the Telstra Gold Phone and it appears our dreams are Telstra's commands. The return of the infamous 80s phone-box has been revealed via the companies Twitter and Facebook page, as well as a teaser page with extra details.

Not only does the phone return with the retro-styled push-button dialpad, but the machine also has a great big  <quote> “follow-on” </quote>  button.

While we could detail the features here, we recommend checking out Telstra's detailed page with all the details here. It's great to see a company go back to its roots, especially when it's as big as Telstra and we look forward to reviewing the phone soon.
